How do I convert image to icon online for free?

Upload one or multiple images, set size and crop, then convert and download.

Yes, PNG images are supported and converted directly to ICO format.

Yes, JPG and JPEG files are supported.

Can I convert multiple images to ICO at once?

Yes, drag and drop multiple files, then use Convert All.

Is ZIP download recommended for batch exports?

Yes, use Download All as ZIP for multi-image output.

Can I download icons one by one?

Yes, each item has its own Download button after conversion.

Which icon sizes are available?

16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128, 256, and 512 are supported.

Does 16x16 favicon export work?

Yes, select 16x16 and convert.

Can I create 512x512 icon?

Yes, 512x512 is available in Size options.

How do crop coordinates work?

Yes, drag inside the crop region to move it.

How do I do free-form cropping?

Select Free Form and drag crop edges/corners or edit width and height values.

What is Original Ratio mode?

It keeps crop ratio matching the source image ratio.

What does Stretch to fit do?

It stretches output to fill selected icon size exactly.

Can I flip and rotate before export?

Yes, rotate left/right and flip horizontal/vertical are available.
