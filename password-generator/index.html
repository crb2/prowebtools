FAQ

How do I use this Password Generator?

1) Set Length with the slider. 2) Set How Many with the stepper. 3) Select character options. 4) Click Generate Passwords and then Copy All when needed.

What are good settings for strong passwords?

Use at least 16 characters and enable uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols for better entropy.

What does Exclude Ambiguous mean?

It removes similar-looking characters such as 0/O and 1/l/I to reduce typing mistakes.

Tips for faster use

Hold stepper up/down for quick count changes, use 16 to 24 length for normal accounts, and use 24+ for critical accounts.

Does this tool have keyboard shortcuts?

No custom shortcuts are required. Standard browser keys work: Tab/Shift+Tab to move focus, Space to toggle focused checkboxes, and Enter to activate focused buttons.

Will my selected Length stay after reload?

Yes. The selected Length and How Many values are saved locally in your browser and restored when you reopen the page.

Are my passwords sent to a server?

No. Passwords are generated in your browser using secure randomness.
