Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ProwebTools collects, uses, and protects information when you access our free online tools for image, video, PDF, and text processing. We are committed to a privacy-aware experience and we design our platform to minimize unnecessary data collection.

When you use ProwebTools, we may collect limited technical information such as browser type, device category, language, approximate location based on IP, and interaction events that help us improve performance. How We Use Information

We use information to operate and secure the website, analyze usage trends, fix errors, prevent abuse, and improve tool quality. We may also use aggregated analytics to understand which pages and keywords bring value to users, so we can improve navigation and SEO discoverability for relevant services.

File Processing and Storage

Many ProwebTools features process files directly in your browser session. For tools that require server-side actions, files are handled only for the requested function and are not retained longer than necessary for processing, security, or legal obligations. We do not sell uploaded file data.

Cookies and Third-Party Services

We may use cookies or local storage for preferences, analytics, and core functionality. We may also use trusted third-party services such as analytics or embedded providers, and those services operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

Your Choices and Rights

You can control cookies through browser settings, avoid submitting personal details in optional forms, and stop using services at any time. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, update, or request deletion of personal data shared with us through direct communication channels.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
